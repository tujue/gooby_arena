Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Gooby Arena collects minimal data:

  • Connection Data: IP address, browser type (for WebRTC connections)
  • Game Data: Player name, customizations (stored locally)
  • Analytics: Page views, session duration (anonymous)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide multiplayer gaming functionality
  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To prevent abuse and maintain fair play

3. Data Storage

Player preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. No personal data is sent to our servers except for multiplayer matchmaking.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for game functionality. You can disable cookies, but this may affect gameplay.

5. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Render.com: Website & multiplayer server hosting

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics
